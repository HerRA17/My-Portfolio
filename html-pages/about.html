Greetings, I'm Hermann, an enthusiastic Full-Stack Developer trainee. My roots trace back to Mexico City, where I was born and raised. Through my journey, I've had the privilege of residing in three diverse countries—Mexico, the USA, and Germany—immersing myself in various cultures and gaining invaluable perspectives. These experiences have instilled a deep sense of gratitude within me. Although my initial academic path was in Biology, with aspirations of becoming a Dentist, I soon realized that my true passion lay elsewhere. After investing a few years in the dental field and completing about two years of Dentistry studies, I came to the profound realization that this wasn't my destined path. This prompted me to embark on a journey of self-discovery, exploring the myriad opportunities that awaited me. This exploration led me down unexpected avenues. I briefly ventured into the domains of Retail and Hospitality, each offering a unique perspective on the world. Eventually, serendipity brought me to the realm of Information Technology (IT), which resonated deeply with me. The pandemic, coupled with challenging familial experiences, served as a catalyst for a shift in my perspective. It underscored the importance of cherishing life and acknowledging our inherent fortune. These events inspired me to embark on a solo endeavor. Drawing inspiration from friends who had successfully established their own companies, I set an ambitious goal for myself: to evolve into a proficient Full-Stack Developer.
