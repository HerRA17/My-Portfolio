Introduction:

The Meet App is an application that will display events in many locations, the user will be able to change the number of events and as well the location, additionally it will display visual aid about the events. The App was build in the Front-End with React/javascript, Enzyme, Jest-Cucumber for the testing part and hosted in Github, and in the Back-End Node/Express as Lambda function (FaaS), hosted in AWS.

Challenges:

The Timeline was expected to be 30 days, Techstack: Node Version Manager was outdated and needed a specific version, Google validating App (Welcome site). Budget was very limited, therefore I used as little from apps or services that charge and free alternatives.
